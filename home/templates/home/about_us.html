About us

Our story

"Row to Grow" began 10 years ago with a simple idea: to provide rowing enthusiasts with the highest-quality products and gear available. Our founders, all passionate rowers themselves, noticed a gap in the market and set goal to fill it up and to provide not just rowing community but whole active community with variety of products.
Starting a business from scratch is never easy, but our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction helped us overcome the challenges we faced. We worked tirelessly to curate a selection of rowing-related products such as: rowing accessories, rowing apperal, rowing books, rowing equipment and many more. Over the years, we've built a reputation for exceptional quality and service, and we've expanded our offerings to include everything from books and clothing to accessories.

Goals

Today we remain committed to our founding vision: to help the rowing community grow by providing the tools and resources that rowers need to succeed. We're proud of what we've achieved so far, and we look forward to continuing to serve our customers for years to come and to expand our area and products we provide you with!

Our founders

Marko Tot

Marko is a former profesional rowing athleete and passionate rowing coach with years of experience in the sport.
His love for rowing has driven him to continue serving the community by offering high-quality rowing products to enthusiasts of all levels. He is still dedicated to the sport and committed to provide top-notch products and services have made him a trusted name in the rowing community. With his expertise and background in the sport, Row to Grow has become a go-to destination for rowing enthusiasts.

Elaine Daly

Elaine Daly is a former rower and coach,she is currently a design instructor at UCC. Her passion for rowing is still evident in her life today, as she continues to be involved in the sport in various ways.
Despite no longer coaching, she remains connected to the rowing community and is always eager to share her knowledge and love for the sport. Elaine is ensuring that the company provides not only exceptional rowing equipment but also aesthetically pleasing and functional products.

Final thoughts

At Row to Grow, we're proud to have Marko and Elaine as our leaders. Their passion for rowing and dedication to the customers is what sets us apart and makes us a trusted name in the rowing community. From beginner rowers to seasoned pros, Row to Grow has everything needed to make the most out of the sport.
