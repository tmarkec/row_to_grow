Shipping & returns

Delivery time?

Our standard delivery time is 3-5 days for Ireland & UK. For other Europe countries up to 10 days, while for countries located outside Europe expected delivery time up to 20 days!

Where we deliver?

Although we are delivering all over the world some delays might happend due to shipping procedures outside Eurozone.

Shipping prices

Our standard shipping rate is 10 euro per order, for all orders above 100 euros shipping is FREE!!!

Returns

You may return unopened or damaged products up to 30 days after receiving them. We hope you love your "Row to grow" products, but if you are in any way unsatisfied, contact us via contact form on the website with your order number as subject. Once we have received your product(s), your payment will be refunded.
